About us

Behineh Sazan Soushiant Co, is a company founded in 1383 RIC and is active in the field of isolation, optimizing energy of buildings and in the area of double glazed UPVC and bulletproof windows and doors

Succeeding together, mutual trust, having economic partners aside us, cooperating with experts and highly skilled consultants and relying on internal strengths where some of the motivations for Behineh Sazane Soushiant Co to start its activities in 1383 RIC in the field of isolation, optimizing energy of the building and specially in the area of double glazed UPVC and bulletproof windows and doors using the most update technologies and standards and with the latest devices and machinery, in cooperation with highly skilled employees.

Our Mission

It is noticed that the trading unit of Behineh Sazane Soushiant Co which is an independent team of expert consultants and engineers, in order to reach the main company goals which is fundraising from internal and external sources to optimize the energy use, has taken new steps forward by using the new generation of thermal break windows, sunergy glass, anti-theft glass and bullet proof glass.

Our Vision

One the main visions of Behineh Sazan Soushiant Co is to strengthen our expert teams in order to start an engineering unit to design products with high quality and according to the latest technologies, and expanding the internal and external markets related to our trading unit.
